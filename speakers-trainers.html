January 11th-13th-Accra,Gh

Speakers

We are happy to invite influential speakers , entreprenuers and skill developers to educate,share ideas and develop skills of individuals on entreprenuership and production. Prepare for an inspiring program.

Brandi Collins

A CEO of a manufacturing company that makes quality and fashionable female dresses and shoes.

" We are surrounded by lots of opportunities. The most important step is the beginning. Young people should step out of their comfort zones and try. They should not let the fear of failure hold them back; with the right attitude and determination, everything is achievable."

Bill Johnson

An American startup providing an e-commerce platform for electronics and computers.

"As an entreprenuer, I think I am defined by how I approach everything in life and the way my mind works. I constantly have an urge to understand situations and find solutions for most problems I encounter. In my mind this quickly translates into ways to have impact- start a business that will employ people,develop valuable products and provide profit for the entreprenuer and investors."

Ursula Burns

CEO of Delta Home Loans that provides mortage products to Ghanaians and operates under the supervision of the Bank of Ghana as a non-bank financial institution.

"Research until you have full confidence in your idea, then be tenacious in driving it through to the end, regardless of the naysayers."

Richard Ansong

A web and mobile application developer and provides a payment platform for banks.

"Entreprenuership comes from within. Your challenges and how you percieve them determine how you take opportunities around you. There's no specific age to be an entreprenuer, but to succed as an entreprenuer you need to know people. So your life should be built around building good relationships with people."
